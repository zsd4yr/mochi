Mochi for the GGJ 2021

--Game Controls--

*Human Player Ritsu:
Movement 
W - Up
S - Down
A - Left
D - Right

E - Interact with objects surrounded by a yellow colored circle 

*Robot Mochi:
Move Ritsu to a terminal with a pink circle around it and/or press 'E'
Respawn Button - Move Mochi to a nearby spawn disc. You can use this at any time to stop and bring back Mochi.
Direction buttons - Queue the directions in which Mochi moves. 
Execute button - Mochi starts independently moving in the specified directions. 

*Misc. Controls:
R - Restart Level
Esc. - Quit Game

*Interactables:

Red & Yellow Pressure Plates can be activated by Mochi moving on them.

Yellow Diamond pressure plates - Permenantly activate or deactivate 
						connected environment object.
Red Square pressure plates - Toggles doors open when moved inside 
						and closed when moved outside them.
